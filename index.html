What are Umamusume?

Umamusume are humanoid girls who possess horse traits and features.
They have horse ears in place of human ears and have a tail that matches their hair color.
They possess incredible speed and stamina, far beyond that of a human and comparable with that of a real-life horse.

"Eclipse first, the rest nowhere"

Eclipse was considered the greatest race horse of his time and the expression then entered the
English vernacular as an expression of dominance
